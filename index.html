History

The art of making the items and crafts by hand is known as Handicrafts.
Indian handicrafts have a rich and diverse history that spans over thousands of years. Handicrafts have been an integral part of India's cultural and economic landscape since ancient times, with evidence of skilled artisans and their work found in the excavated remains of the Indus Valley Civilization (3300 BCE - 1300 BCE).
Throughout India's long and varied history, handicrafts have played an important role in the country's economy, providing livelihoods for countless artisans, and showcasing the country's rich artistic heritage. The tradition of handicrafts in India is deeply intertwined with the country's social and cultural fabric and has been shaped by a variety of influences, including religion, geography, and trade.
